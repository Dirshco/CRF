Dear Members of the Contributions Committee,

I am writing to you on behalf of the Joint Youth Education Committee to formally submit our funding request for the upcoming 2026 Summer Youth Initiative. In collaboration with our local partner, the Sudanese Association of Greater Lansing (SAGL), we are building a supportive higher education and career pipeline tailored specifically to first-generation and immigrant families in our Lansing communities.

By leveraging the formal 501(c)(3) tax status and infrastructure of The Combined Research Foundation alongside the direct community access and cultural integration managed by SAGL, this initiative provides area students with debt-free college financing toolkits and direct career mentorship paths. We are seeking a corporate sponsorship of $500 to fund bilingual scholarship booklets and student academic supplies.

Thank you for your ongoing investment in the youth and educational accessibility of Ingham County. We look forward to your consideration of our proposal.

Sincerely,
