Telehealth Research & Healthcare Transformation

The Combined Research Foundation is dedicated to accelerating the integration of digital infrastructure within clinical practice environments. Our active trials analyze how asynchronous technologies and ambient metrics optimize patient outcomes and reduce clinical burnout.

Through our dedicated telehealth initiatives, we analyze data trends from community care centers, focusing on long-term outcomes in chronic symptom monitoring and senior cognitive care support models.

Active Research Frameworks

1. Ambient Clinical Scribe Optimization

Investigating the accuracy, linguistic mapping, and template synthesis of AI recorders capturing continuous patient-physician interactions in sub-acute and long-term care environments.

2. Mobile Nociplastic Pain Tracking

Evaluating mobile interface adoption among elderly patient populations tracking localized and centralized pain markers, testing layout accessibility settings for older users.

3. Continuous Glycemic Architecture

Analyzing systemic integration protocols for continuous data telemetry within post-acute nursing facilities to safely minimize acute glycemic variance events.
