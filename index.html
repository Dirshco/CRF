About the Combined Research Foundation

The Combined Research Foundation (CRF) is a nonprofit organization based in Michigan dedicated to empowering the next generation of healthcare professionals. We are committed to addressing the evolving needs of a changing global population by focusing on key areas such as geriatrics, mental health, and complex patient care.

Our Story

The world is experiencing a significant demographic shift. As our population of older adults grows, so does the prevalence of chronic conditions and mental health disorders. This creates an increasing demand on healthcare systems and makes patient care more complex, especially for individuals managing multiple health issues.

At the Combined Research Foundation, we see this challenge as an opportunity. By investing in innovative programs and nurturing bright, compassionate minds, we can shape a new era of medical excellence. Our goal is to equip future healthcare leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to not only meet these challenges but to pioneer transformative solutions.

Join us on this journey to build a healthier tomorrow. Your passion and dedication, combined with our vision, can create a powerful synergy that propels healthcare forward.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of healthcare professionals in both clinical and research areas. We help them excel in the U.S. healthcare system by championing innovative approaches like telehealth, artificial intelligence and transformative care models.

Our Vision

We envision a future where recent graduates are not just prepared for their careers but are also at the forefront of improving public health. We will achieve this by investing in innovative programs that address critical healthcare needs, particularly in geriatrics, mental health, and dental care.

The Tele-Geriatric Research Fellowship Program

Pioneering the Future of Geriatric Care

The global population is aging rapidly, creating an urgent need for innovative solutions in geriatric healthcare. This unique fellowship is at the forefront of this change, training the next generation of healthcare professionals to address the complex challenges faced by older adults through the power of telehealth.

What is the Program?

This immersive, year-long experience blends advanced clinical training with cutting-edge research. Fellows will work alongside leading experts to:

  • Conduct impactful research on telehealth's role in improving health outcomes for older adults.
  • Develop expertise in managing complex geriatric conditions, including multimorbidity and mental health issues.
  • Innovate and implement new telehealth models that enhance patient access and quality of care.
  • Present their findings at national conferences and publish in peer-reviewed journals.

Who Should Apply?

We are seeking passionate and driven recent graduates from medical, dental, and allied health fields who are eager to make a significant impact. Ideal candidates have a strong interest in geriatrics, technology, and a commitment to research.
