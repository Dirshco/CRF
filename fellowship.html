Independent Tele-Geriatric Research Fellowship

The Combined Research Foundation (CRF) offers an elite, independent fellowship framework crafted for ambitious medical graduates and healthcare professionals. Our mission is to bridge the traditional clinical space with cutting-edge medical informatics, operational workflows, and healthcare data science systems.

If you are exploring competitive options for clinical training, our independent research fellowship provides unmatched mentorship under senior clinical faculty, offering full transition pathways into the United States healthcare ecosystem.

Core Curriculum & Research Domains

Fellows do not simply observe; they drive multi-platform digital research initiatives from concept to conference presentation. Training pathways include:

  • ✓
    Clinical Informatics & Ambient AI: Developing and validating tools that synthesize ambient clinical encounters into structured electronic health records.
  • ✓
    Geriatric Digital Infrastructure: Deploying remote monitoring, fall prevention systems, and accessible user interfaces customized for senior populations.
  • ✓
    Biostatistics & Literature Meta-Analysis: Processing massive national clinical datasets using rigorous Python and R workflows for systematic reviews.
