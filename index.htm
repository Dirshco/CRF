About

The Combined Research Foundation is a nonprofit organization that was founded in Michigan, USA.

Background

The global population is undergoing a significant demographic shift. The number of older adults is rapidly increasing, accompanied by a rising prevalence of chronic conditions and mental health disorders. This demographic transformation poses substantial challenges to healthcare systems worldwide.

Simultaneously, the complexity of patient care has escalated due to the increasing incidence of multimorbidity, where individuals manage multiple chronic conditions concurrently. This convergence of factors necessitates innovative and comprehensive approaches to healthcare delivery to address the evolving needs of this population.

As a pioneering force in healthcare education, we aim to cultivate the brightest talents and minds for the future. Our organization, nestled in the heart of Michigan, is dedicated to shaping a new era of medical excellence and compassion.

Join us on this transformative journey towards a healthier tomorrow. Let your passion and dedication merge with our vision, creating a synergy that propels healthcare forward.

Mission

To empower the next generation of healthcare professionals in both clinical and research areas, and to excel in the U.S. healthcare system using both transformation and telehealth.

Vision

To enhance the next generation of healthcare professionals by investing in innovative programs that address critical healthcare needs. We envision a future where recent graduates excel in their careers and contribute significantly to improving the health and well-being of our population, especially in the areas of geriatrics, mental health, and dental care.
